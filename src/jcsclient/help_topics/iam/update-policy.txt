
NAME:
    update-policy


DESCRIPTION:
    Updates an existing policy for your JCS account.


SYNOPSIS:
    update-policy --name <value> | --id <value> --policy-document <value> 


OPTIONS:
    --name (string)
    The name of the policy.

    --id (string)
    The id of the policy.

    --policy-document (string)
    The policy document for the policy

EXAMPLES:
    jcs iam update-policy --name Policy123 --policy-document "{\"name\": \"a1bc2443\", \"statement\": [{\"action\": [\"jrn:jcs:dss:ListBucket\"], \"resource\": [\"jrn:jcs:dss:012345678901:Bucket:Bucket234\"], \"effect\": \"allow\"}]}"
	
	jcs iam update-policy --id 1a4e789ac85c985b3495f3495743a5e8 --policy-document "{\"name\": \"a1bc2443\", \"statement\": [{\"action\": [\"jrn:jcs:dss:ListBucket\"], \"resource\": [\"jrn:jcs:dss:012345678901:Bucket:Bucket234\"], \"effect\": \"allow\"}]}"
